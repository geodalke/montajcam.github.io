Montaj Cam

A Minimalist Video Camera App For Creating Montages

Quick Start

  1. Choose a camera: Ultra, Wide, Tele, or Selfie.
  2. Touch and hold the circle to Record a video clip. Release to end recording and automatically add the clip to the Montaj.
  3. After any camera recording, the background contains a freeze frame and the following functions are enabled:
    • Background: Interactively move, zoom, and rotate the freeze frame for the best composition.
    • Spotlight: Highlight a region of the background. Interactively move, zoom, and adjust aspect ratio.
    • Cameo: Insert a live video selfie window. Interactively move and zoom the window.
  4. A freeze frame from the previous camera recording is used as a background, unlocking Background, Spotlight, and Cameo functions.
  5. Proceed with any combination of Cameras, Background, Spotlight, or Cameo in any order, as often as you want. Montaj is saved with smooth transitions between the clips.
