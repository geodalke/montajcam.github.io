Montages

A montage is a sequence of short video clips edited together to tell a story, compress time, or highlight contrast. It’s one of the most effective tools in visual storytelling, used to evoke emotion or deliver meaning quickly.

Traditionally, montages require recording many clips, organizing them, and editing them by hand. This process takes time, tools, and an attempt to re-create a moment that is gone.

Montaj

Montaj records clips live, one by one, and builds the montage as you go. The app handles the transitions and timing—there is no editing. You focus on the moments; Montaj shapes the story.

Minimalist App

The interface is a single screen so you stay focused on recording, not navigating menus. You are always in the live moment. There is no final editing, just share and move on to the next event.
