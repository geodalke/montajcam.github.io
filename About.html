Mindhive, Inc. — Technology & Montaj (Overview)

Advanced, patent-backed video communication concepts validated through a focused consumer app (Montaj). Intended for technical partners exploring mission-critical verticals.

Core Technology

Mindhive has developed a portfolio of patented technologies that reimagine how video is created, shared, and used in immersive contexts.

  • Immersive interaction (AR/VR): Move beyond post-editing — users interact directly with captured content in a shared environment.
  • Granular security & privacy: Per-user access controls and mission-focused formatting that preserve privacy while enabling collaboration.
  • Independent perspectives: Each participant maintains their own view, emphasis, and metadata in a shared scene.
  • Scalable feed clustering: Manage and format many simultaneous feeds — applicable to drone clusters, distributed field teams, and multi-unit operations.

Target problems solved

Deliver reliable, secure, and efficient situational awareness where speed, trust, and the ability to preserve multiple viewpoints matter most.

Patents & IP Foundational claims for group interaction, privacy, and viewpoint management

Montaj — Proof of Concept

A minimalist social app that embodies Mindhive principles and validates our no-edit, immersive workflow in a real-world product.

  • No-edit workflow: Capture, arrange, and publish without traditional trimming or post-processing.
  • Rapid build: Conceived, developed, and launched in a few months using Mindhive tech.
  • Live validation: Demonstrates usability, performance, and UX patterns applicable to advanced deployments.
Why Montaj matters

It provides credibility, a usable testbed, and a smaller-scope product to iterate quickly while we prepare for mission-critical verticals.

Vertical Markets & Next Steps

With validated concepts and patent protection, Mindhive is positioned to partner with engineering teams to develop tailored systems for:

  • First responders: Fast, private, mission-optimized communication where each responder keeps an independent evidentiary view.
  • Drone clusters: Aggregation, secure distribution, and mission formatting of many simultaneous feeds.
  • Secure group networks: Industrial, security, and enterprise deployments requiring per-user preferences and strict access control.

We are seeking technical partners to co-develop prototypes and production systems that integrate Mindhive IP with domain-specific hardware and workflows.
